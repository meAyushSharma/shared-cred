Critical: Exporting Keys

You MUST export your crypto-keys from the dashboard. These are ZIP files containing your local private keys. Without them, your data is unrecoverable on new browsers or devices.

Security Deep Dive

Why trust Credential Manager?

We use End-to-End Encryption (E2EE). Your data is encrypted in the browser before storage. We never touch your 'private key'. You, and those you explicitly authorize, are the only ones who can ever decipher the values.

How does encryption work?

We use Symmetric Key distribution combined with Proxy Re-Encryption. Your credential value is locked with a symmetric key, which is then wrapped in your Public Key. The Private Key stays in your browser's IndexDB, never hitting our servers.

How does sharing work?

We implement Relationship Based Access Control (ReBAC). You can grant roles like Viewer (view only), Editor (edit only), or Author (delete permissions). Access can be revoked instantly without compromising the master security of the vault.
